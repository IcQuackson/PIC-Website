The requirements document turns the idea into something testable. It fixes the stack around an offline Wi-Fi mesh with BATMAN-adv and local Push-To-Talk voice, then adds hard field constraints for the airborne relay and ground nodes.

The numbers are the useful part: the UAV payload must stay below **500 g**, support at least **30 minutes** of mission time, and preserve link quality above **-75 dBm RSSI** and **20 dB SNR**, while median RTT stays under **50 ms** and packet loss under **10%**.
